# Refund Policy

**Effective date:** [insert date]

## TL;DR

| You did                                                                       | You get                                                                                       |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cancelled within the 14-day free trial                                        | Nothing was charged; nothing to refund                                                        |
| Cancelled a monthly plan                                                      | Service runs until the end of the current month, then stops. No refund for the current month. |
| Cancelled an annual plan within 30 days of purchase                           | Pro-rata refund (days unused / 365 × annual price)                                            |
| Cancelled an annual plan after 30 days                                        | Service runs until annual renewal date, then stops. No refund.                                |
| Found Jobab couldn't connect to Meta because we never got App Review approved | Full refund of any amount paid                                                                |
| Found a billing error (double charge, wrong tier)                             | Full refund of the disputed amount within 5 business days                                     |

---

## 1. Free trial

You get **14 days free** when you sign up. No card required.

If you don't add a payment method by day 14, the Service stops
automatically. You won't be charged.

---

## 2. Monthly subscriptions

Monthly fees are charged in advance on the billing date each month.

- **Cancel any time** from your dashboard or by emailing hello@jobab.com
- Service continues until the end of the paid month
- No refund for the partial month you used
- No future months will be charged

---

## 3. Annual subscriptions

Annual fees are charged in advance, on a 12-month cycle. Annual plans
get a discount vs paying monthly.

- **First 30 days:** full pro-rata refund available. Cancel, get back
  `(365 - days used) / 365 × annual price`.
- **After 30 days:** no refund, but the Service continues until the
  paid year ends.
- You can choose to auto-renew or not on your account page.

---

## 4. Refunds for Service problems

We will refund the affected period if:

- **Service was down** for more than 12 consecutive hours (excluding
  planned maintenance announced 48h+ in advance)
- **A bug caused customer-facing harm** (e.g. AI sent the wrong
  customer's order to someone else)
- **You couldn't use the integration** because of an issue on our side
  (not Meta's side)

Email hello@jobab.com with the affected dates and we'll process within
5 business days.

---

## 5. Refunds NOT covered

We do not refund for:

- **Customer behaviour** — if a customer didn't buy something the AI
  recommended, that's normal sales conversion, not a service failure
- **Meta policy changes** — if Meta changes API rules and an automation
  stops working, we'll migrate as fast as possible but we can't refund
  the time it takes
- **Your decision to disconnect a page** mid-billing-cycle on a monthly
  plan
- **WhatsApp message charges** — these are Meta's costs passed through;
  once Meta delivers the message we can't claw it back

---

## 6. WhatsApp template fees

Separate from your subscription. These are usage-based, billed to your
account in arrears.

- **Service messages** (inside 24h customer window): free
- **Utility / marketing / authentication templates** (outside 24h):
  billed per Meta's rates + a 10% administration fee

If you dispute a template charge, email hello@jobab.com within 30 days
of the invoice. We'll investigate and refund if our records show an
error.

---

## 7. How to request a refund

Email **hello@jobab.com** with:

- Your account email
- The billing date(s) you're disputing
- A brief explanation

We aim to respond within 2 business days and process valid refunds
within 5 business days. Refunds go back to the original payment method.

For bKash refunds: please allow up to 7 business days as bKash settlements
can be slower than card refunds.

---

## 8. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or card issuer without first
contacting us, your account may be suspended pending review. We almost
always prefer to resolve disputes directly — it's faster for you and
keeps your account in good standing.
