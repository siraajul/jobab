# Outreach Templates

## DM / Voice note (Bangla — recommended)

> Apa/Bhai, Assalamu alaikum. Ami [Name], Jobab namok ekta AI assistant
> toiri korchhi je apnar moto Facebook Page seller der DM gulo handle
> kore — auto reply, order create, sob kichu. Apnar product gulo
> beshi sundor, ami onek din dhore apnar Page follow korchhi.
>
> Ekta 30-min phone-e kotha bolar shomoy ache? Aamra 3 jon merchant er
> shathe 4 shoptaher pilot run korbo — bilkul free, apnar shathe ekdin
> boshbo, ekta kichu hole apni nije control nibe parben. Apnar
> opinion-tao chai eta improve korar jonno.
>
> Schedule: [calendly link]

## English version (for fluent merchants)

> Assalamu alaikum / Hello,
>
> I'm building an AI assistant for Bangladeshi social-commerce sellers —
> it handles customer DMs, takes orders, and lets the merchant step in
> when needed. Saw your Page and your stuff looks great.
>
> I'm running a 4-week pilot with 3 merchants (free; you can stop
> any time) and would love to chat about your DM workflow before
> deciding whether you'd be a fit. 30 min on the phone.
>
> [calendly link]

## Why each line is there

- **Personal compliment about their store** — proves we looked
- **Specific number "3 merchants"** — implies selectivity, raises status
- **"Free; stop any time"** — neutralises the cost concern
- **30 min, not 60** — lower commitment cost
- **Calendly link** — removes scheduling back-and-forth

## What NOT to do

- Don't say "free trial" — implies paid product later, raises a flag
- Don't say "AI bot" — too generic, sounds like a thousand failed
  ChatBoss / Chatfuel pitches
- Don't lead with the dashboard / demo — they'll dismiss it sight-unseen
- Don't promise "save X hours" before you know their volume
