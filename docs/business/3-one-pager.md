# One-pager — investor teaser

A single-page summary for cold investor outreach. The deck is for the
meeting; this is what gets you the meeting.

::: tip How to use
Copy the block below into a Google Doc, format as A4 portrait, export to
PDF. Attach to the first email. Keep it under 400 words on the page so it
stays scannable.
:::

---

## Cold-email template

When sending the one-pager:

```
Subject: Jobab — AI shop assistant for BD merchants (pre-seed)

Hi [Name],

I'm building Jobab — an AI that runs the customer-DM side of a
Bangladeshi shop owner's business. Replies in Bangla, recognises
products from photos, takes orders, sends bKash links.

Quick stats:
  • Full product working, documented at siraajul.github.io/jobab
  • [N] BD merchants interviewed, [N] committed to pilot
  • Raising $400K to onboard the first 100 merchants

Attached: 1-page summary. 3-min demo: [Loom link].

Worth a 30-min call this week or next?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Keep it under 100 words.** Investors get hundreds of these. Short +
attachment wins.

---

## What to update before sending

- `[N]` placeholders → real numbers from merchant interviews
- `[date]` placeholders → actual Meta submission dates
- `[Your background]` → 1 line, e.g. "5 years at [company], built [thing]"
- `[Loom link]` → the 3-min demo video
- Both photo placeholders in the deck → real BD merchant photo
